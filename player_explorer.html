Understanding years_exp in Sleeper:
• 0 = rookie in their first active NFL season (drafted this year)
• null / blank = pre-draft / offseason entrant — not yet in the NFL, or between contracts
• 1 = second-year player (eligible for taxi squad if your rules allow it)
• 2+ = veteran

Recommended rookie draft pool rule options:
• Conservative: years_exp === 0 only (current-season confirmed rookies)
• Moderate: years_exp === 0 OR (null AND age ≤ 23) — catches pre-draft players
• Broad: years_exp ≤ 1 AND age ≤ 24 — includes 2nd-year players still on taxi
